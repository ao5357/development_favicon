Regex Pattern

Regex stands for "Regular Expressions", which is a means to match stuff. There are different flavors of regular expression, and this extension uses the JavaScript one. I recommend trying out your patterns at RegexPal.

The quick things to know about regex match patterns are:

  • The patterns have to match the URL or title of the tab to be applied
  • .* matches anything, so you usually want to start and end your pattern with it
  • The dots and slashes in patterns have to be escaped, by preceding them with a backslash (ie. http:\/\/)
  • You can do a lot with regular expressions. That's why we use them
Effect

Effects are the way the favicon gets modified:

  • top: puts a colored stripe over the top quarter
  • right: puts a colored stripe over the right quarter
  • bottom: puts a colored stripe over the bottom quarter
  • left: puts a colored stripe over the left quarter
  • cover: covers the whole area in semi-transparent color
  • replace: replaces non-transparent parts of the icon with color
  • background: color fill of the whole area behind the icon
  • xor-top: like 'top', but whites out what gets covered
Color

The color used by the applied effect (see above).

Ordering

The first (in the list) pattern that matches gets applied. You can drag-and-drop to re-order the rows above to give them priority.
